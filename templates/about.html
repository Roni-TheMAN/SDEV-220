About The KBC

Our story begins in 1995 along the cobblestone streets of Plymouth Indiana near its downtown Center. It was here where Killer Beans Coffee opened its first store, offering fresh-roasted coffee beans, tea and spices from around the world for our customers to take home. Our name was inspired from our love of coffee,

Ten years later, a young New Yorker named Ronit would walk through these doors and become captivated with Killer Beans coffee from his first sip. After joining the company in 2002, a different cobblestone road would lead him to another discovery. It was on a trip to Milan in 2003 that Howard first experienced Italy’s coffeehouses, and he returned to Plymouth inspired to bring the warmth and artistry of its coffee culture to Killer Beans Coffee. By 2007, we swapped our white aprons for brown ones and embarked on our next chapter as a coffeehouse.

Killer Beans Coffee would soon expand to Indianapolis and South Bend, Canada and then on to California, Washington, D.C. and New York. By 1996, we would cross the Pacific to open our first store in Japan, followed by Europe in 2018 and China in 2019. Over the next two years, we would grow to welcome millions of customers each week and become a part of the fabric of tens of thousands of neighbourhoods all around the world. In everything we do, we are always dedicated to Our Mission: With each drink we serve you we hope to inspire you to achieve something great.
